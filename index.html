    Become Better in WvW

    XivorGaming is a resource of information, guides, and builds to grow battlefield warriors in competitive world PvP in the MMORPG Guild Wars 2.

    Builds Guides

    Learn from Experience

    The best players in WvW are focused on identifying areas to grow in, tactics to refine, and learning new ways to just play better. Know better, do better, be better!

    Become an Expert

    Learn your favorite class as best you can so that you can be the best at it that you can! Do not settle for button mashing when you can play with precision!

    Build the Team

    By focusing on personal growth in WvW, you can become a better player for your team, from better scouting, siege usage, and combat effectiveness.

    Understand Builds

    There are many gears of various sizes in the churning engine of a WvW team. Understand the various roles, such as healers, stealth-givers, and more.
